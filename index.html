    Quality Public Initiatives mission is to create societies that are equipped in dealing with issues that affect communities that are impoverished both nationally and internationally.

    Quality Public Initiatives addresses this by recruting the best hands, whom are passionate about public healthcare and are determined to ensure that they make a change. We ensure that they get the best training and implored with the proper materials needed to make a difference in the school, district or community they are placed. The goal is to work hand in hand in ensuring that every community in Liberia can boast of adequate healthcare, schools and daily meals. All this done through the Quality Public Initiatives program led by its CEO Charles Dean Wilson

    WHAT WE DO?

    Quality Public Initiatives addresses public health issues by launching different initiatives which help specific communities in the East Coast of the United States and the West Coast of Africa. QHI provides educational materials, food incentives. Lastly education on public health issues such as; sexual protection,contraceptives and alcohol/drug prevention.
