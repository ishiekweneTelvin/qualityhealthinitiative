    Quality Health Initiatives mission is to create a society that is equiped in dealing with the issues that affect Liberia and aspires to build a future that all Liberians can be proud of.

    Quality Health Initiatives addresses the by recruting the best hands, whom are passionate about public healthcare and are determined to ensure that they make a change. We ensure that they get the best training and implored with the proper materials needed to make a difference in the school, district or community they are placed. The goal is to work hand in hand in ensuring that evry community in Liberia can boast of adequate healthcare, schools and daily meals. All this done through the Quality Health Initiatives program led by its CEO Charles Dean Wilson

    WHAT WE DO?

    Recruit:

    Quality Health Initiatives looks out for the best hands for the job. Each person tailored for the task ahead

    Train:

    After Recruitment, each recruit undergoes training to learn what it takes to be a part of Quality Health Initiatives

    Place:

    Fellows are placed at differect parts of the community with emphasis on where they can make the most impact

    Ongoing support:

    Fellows attend a pre-service institute to learn the relevant skills and methods to be exceptional teachers
